This file should give you an overview over things not working properly or are restricted in usage.

1. Not yet tested:
   - all RDF routines!
   - GE (Gibbs) Ensemble, since chemPot calculation was not yet verified (even for rigid molecules)


2. Restricted to rigid molecules:
   - Calculation of transportproperties
   - Calculation of the chemical potential through Widom or GradIns


3. Known issues/bugs:
   - bad sampling in MC, especially for smaller molecules, since most of the moves/rotates are unit specific
   - Ewald with MC results with high pressure (bug or bad model? yet to be verified)
   - all routines using "mol2unit" are not working correctly
   - mol2unit sets the unit orientation by multiplying the molecule orientation
     with the initial unit orientation instead of the current one


4. Not yet implemtend (coming soon, hopefully :-) ):
   - QShake for constraining bonds to increase time step 
     (by setting it back to equilibrium state in a few iterations)
   - CBMC (configurational bias monte-carlo) for calculation of the chemical potential
     (will also be replacing the current internal moves/rotates, instead moves/rotates of alternative configurations)



Found a bug or having problems with the IDF version? Please contact Michael Schappals (michael.schappals(at)mv.uni-kl.de).
